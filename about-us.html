About Foundation

Our Foundation works to empower youth and children in rural areas through knowledge action and skill building courses. We have collaborated with Tata institute of social science to empower the students in rural areas by providing quality course material. We work towards promoting social entrepreneurship and creating employment opportunities in the rural areas.

Why we do?

Study shows that 60% of the graduates from India have a severe skill mismatch from their industry. According to the Employability Survey 2019 more than 80% engineers lack present-day skills which are required by the market. There is a need of action and knowledge based skills among the youth today to work in any industry. We make such contemporary skills based courses available for the students in rural area so that they can easily access and boost the employment in rural areas.

Our Vision & Mission

We aim to bring empowerment in rural children and youth by offering knowledge and action based skills. Our mission is to provide quality education to every doorstep in rural areas. We aim at creating employment opportunities for the rural youths by giving them access to contemporary skills and quality education and linking classroom learning to actual practice and fieldwork projects.
