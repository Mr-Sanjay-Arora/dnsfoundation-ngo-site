What we Do?

We offer high standard teaching and research opportunities by linking classroom learning to actual practice and fieldwork projects. We as a nonprofit organization provide online and offline skill based training to rural youth and we also assist them with placement opportunities. We collaborate with state and non-state actors in enhancing the capacities and skills of the front-line government functionaries such as School Teachers, Official Of Local Self Government, Anganwadi Workers.
